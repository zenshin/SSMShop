Our company

HISTORY | HERITAGE

SUITSURMESUR® is a London | Paris Made To Measure label specialising in luxury couture for the contemporary man of exquisite taste. The brand was created by Monsieur Dike to bring its unique custom suiting philosophy to a new generation of well dressed masses blending traditional tailoring with modern style. Our aesthetic roots are simultaneously in modern tailoring, extracted and inspired by a concept that draws inspiration from our African Heritage. In a market where a suit body lining is traditionally homogenous and monopolised by western designs, we work hard to demonstrate to the world that African arts and prints can be put to good use cross culture especially in the construction of a corporate suit. This legacy of strong cultural identity has been emphasised on extensively in the creation of SUITSURMESUR® Made To Measure collection.

THE VISION

WE ENDEAVOUR TO SHOW THAT OUR CONCEPT CAN BE NO LESS INTERESTING OR BEAUTIFUL

We take pride in rewriting the traditional rules of fashion through the use of artworks, exotic patterns, and prints in our fabric linings with each piece having a backstory. Our garments symbolise imagination, creativity and the link between different forms of art and culture.

OUR MISSION

SOMEONE HAS TO MAKE THINGS BETTER AND IT MIGHT AS WELL BE US

In the corporate world, a lot of people wear suits that are tailored in the same spirit, often too traditional yet lacking the exotic appeal, soul or character that embraces our social diversity. The concept was born out of the need to allow style conscious individuals the opportunity to assert their uniqueness yet creating high quality masterpieces that fit all body curvature and natural posture sans exception.

ETHICS

ARTISANAL APPROACH & NATURAL ABILITIES ARE STILL WITHIN OUR CONFINES

The personalised cutting of fabric, stitching, sewing and skills are all natural requisite aptitude employed in the creation of a Made To Measure tailored suit. These skills that were once acquired over many years through diligent apprenticeship are slowly falling into decline due in part to an ever-evolving industrialised society.

Fortunately this artisanal approach and natural abilities are still within our confines, something that mass production settings can never emulate, whether in terms of construction, quality or character and allow each of our Made To Measure suit to have and benefit from a personalised unique soft touch.

PERSONALISED CUSTOM SERVCIE

UNIQUENESS & STYLE THAT REPOSES ON STRONG CULTURAL VALUE

The nature of SUITSURMESUR service is based not only on the opulent fabrics and custom made pieces that make it the most sought-after and premier service in its kind, but also on its foundations which repose firmly on its unique style that allow us to create a clothing accoutrements that is accustomed to our clients personalities.

Our clients are also welcome to step through our doors for our exclusive services.

FOUNDER & CREATIVE DIRECTOR

STORY TELLING IS IMPORTANT TO US

Jean Louis O. DIKE (Monsieur Dike ) graduated with a distinction in Commercial & International Law (Masters of Laws), Financial Services Law Specialist but could not resist pursuing his long time passion for quality tailored suits. He drew motivation to create special to create special from purchasing off the rack suits proposing little to no design & body lining choices that embrace our social diversity. In a market where a suit body lining is traditionally homogenous and monopolised by western designs, he saw a need to create his own Custom Made suit label with a design that draws inspiration from his multi-cultural (African & European) heritage. With the novel suiting philosophy in operation, Monsieur Dike offers a custom craftsmanship that delivers good fit, great quality and achieves almost the similar level of precise tailoring and attention to detail as Savile Row Bespoke yet reasonably priced to reflect uniqueness.
