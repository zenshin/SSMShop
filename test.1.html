Our company

SUITSURMESUR is a Made To Measure clothing label specialising in luxury couture for the contemporary man of exquisite taste. Our aesthetic roots are simultaneously in modern tailoring, extracted and inspired by a concept that draws inspiration from our African heritage. This legacy of strong cultural identity has been emphasised on widely in the creation of SUITSURMESUR Made To Measure Collection.

WE ENDEAVOUR TO SHOW THAT OUR CONCEPT CAN BE NO LESS INTERESTING OR BEAUTIFUL

We take pride in rewriting the traditional rules of fashion through the use of artworks, exotic patterns, and prints in our fabric linings with each piece having a backstory. Our garments symbolise imagination, creativity and the link between different forms of art and culture.

SOMEONE HAS TO MAKE THINGS BETTER AND IT MIGHT AS WELL BE US

In the corporate world, a lot of people wear suits that are tailored in the same spirit, often too traditional yet lacking the exotic appeal, soul or character that embraces our social diversity. The concept was born out of the need to allow style conscious individuals the opportunity to assert their uniqueness yet creating high quality masterpieces that fit all body curvature and natural posture sans exception.

ARTISANAL APPROACH & NATURAL ABILITIES ARE STILL WITHIN OUR CONFINES

The personalised cutting of fabric, stitching, sewing and skills are all natural requisite aptitude employed in the creation of a Made To Measure tailored suit. These skills that were once acquired over many years through diligent apprenticeship are slowly falling into decline due in part to an ever-evolving industrialised society.

Fortunately this artisanal approach and natural abilities are still within our confines, something that mass production settings can never emulate, whether in terms of construction, quality or character and allow each of our Made To Measure suit to have and benefit from a personalised unique soft touch.

UNIQUENESS & STYLE THAT REPOSE ON STRONG CULTURAL VALUE

The nature of SUITSURMESUR service is based not only on the opulent fabrics and custom made pieces that make it the most sought-after and premier service in its kind, but also on its foundations which repose firmly on its unique style that allow us to create a clothing accoutrements that is accustomed to our clients personalities.

Further, is our willingness to provide a door-to-door personalised delivery of your Custom Made piece at your convenience. Our clients are also welcome to step through our doors for our exclusive services.

THE BRAND

WHO ARE WE?

STORY TELLING IS IMPORTANT TO US

SUITSURMESUR® is a London | Paris Made To Measure brand created by Monsieur Dike to bring its unique suiting philosophy to a new generation of well dressed masses. In a market where a suit body lining is traditionally homogenous and monopolised by western designs, we work hard to demonstrate to the world that African arts and prints can be put to good use cross culture especially in the construction of a corporate suit.

WE MAKE EXQUISITE CLOTHING FOR THE AUDACIOUS GENTLEMAN. IT IS A COMMON ENOUGH ASPIRATION, BUT ONE SELDOM ACCOMPLISHED WITH ELEGANCE

Our cosmopolitan approach gives an alternative to a rather traditional tailoring and organically draws upon mutual exchange of ideas especially one that transcends frontiers. In a nutshell our concept is inspired by the notion ‘make it unique, exotic yet significant’ using excellent fabrics and employing the artisanal craftsmanship of skilled tailors to produce a veritable work of art.

FOUNDER & CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Jean Louis O. DIKE graduated with a distinction in Commercial & International Law (Masters of Laws) Financial Services Law Specialist but could not resist pursuing his long time passion for quality tailored suits. He drew motivation from personal challenges wearing off the rack suits and consequently curated his own Custom Made suit label. Coming from a perspective where he had issues finding a ready to wear brand that offered a perfect fit sans compromise proved difficult. Problems such as suit jacket looking too wide and structured in the shoulder causing unnecessary strained upper arm creases due to his broad shoulders, suit jacket bunching in the under-collar back area, creating folds, break in the lapel, excessive pulling on button stance, causing wrinkled lines radiating outward when suit jacket is buttoned, suit trousers appearing too tight in the thigh, exorbitant alteration costs; all of which presented a real challenge for tailoring in the real world. Safe in the knowledge that Savile Row traditional tailored suits seemed at the time out of reach, he decided to find an alternative to Savile Row craftsmanship by offering a custom service that delivers good fit, great quality and achieves almost the similar level of precise tailoring as Savile Row Bespoke yet reasonable priced to reflect uniqueness.
